In the home page you can choose three different interaction:
  • Visitor
  • Curator
  • User Guide
If you choose Visitor you can choose you Visitor Id and:
  • See your visit path with the order of Point Of Interest you visited
  • See how many time you visited every Point Of Interest
If you choose Curator you can:
  • Visit Summary Groups/Individual> choose the group id and the visitor id to see:
    • Which Point Of Interest was visited and when
    • Which Presentation was played, when and if it was interrupted by the system or the user
    • How many time spent in total in front of a Point Of Interest, relatively to the group average time
    • How many time spent in total listening a Presentation, relatively to the group average time
    • Rate of a Presentation, relatively to the group average rating
  • Museum Statistics> after a while(caused by the number of operations to do) it display the statistics of the museum of:
    • Number of Visitors in the museum for every hour
    • Number of Visitors in a Room for every hour
    • Attraction power: % of Visitors that spent time in front of a Point Of Interest
    • Holding power: total amount of time spent in front of a Point Of Interest, obtained summing time spent of every visitor
